Ina Nersesian was born on the 4th of August, 1990, Republic of Georgia. Since her childhood, she was interested in photography. In 2014 she graduated from Shota Rustaveli Film and Theatre University of Georgia, Major in Cinematography. The same year she became the UN special prize winner of the International Festival "GoldenEye". In 2017, a full scholarship holder of Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Program “Viewfinder”. While graduating MA in 2019, she had an opportunity to make a short fiction film, “Whisper”, as a director and cinematographer in the same time.
